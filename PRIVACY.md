# Privacy Policy - Centrale UCL Manager

## Introduction

Centrale UCL Manager is designed with privacy and GDPR compliance as core principles. This document outlines our data processing practices and your rights as a data subject.

## Data We Collect

### Member Information
- **Personal Data**: Name, email address, phone number, student ID
- **Membership Data**: Role assignments, membership status, circle affiliations
- **Academic Data**: Student ID, graduation year (if applicable)

### Event Participation
- **Registration Data**: Event attendance, ticket purchases
- **Payment Data**: Transaction records (processed via Stripe)
- **Check-in Data**: Event attendance timestamps

### Communication
- **Email Preferences**: Subscription status, communication history
- **Audit Data**: Login times, IP addresses, actions performed

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Legitimate Interest**: Managing association membership and activities
- **Contract**: Providing services you've requested (event tickets, etc.)
- **Consent**: Marketing communications (you can opt-out anytime)
- **Legal Obligation**: Financial record keeping, audit requirements

## How We Use Your Data

- **Member Management**: Role assignments, directory services
- **Event Organization**: Registration, ticketing, attendance tracking
- **Communication**: Newsletters, event notifications, important updates
- **Financial Management**: Processing payments, expense reimbursements
- **Analytics**: Improving our services (anonymized data only)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only when:
- **Service Providers**: Stripe (payments), email service providers
- **Legal Requirements**: Compliance with laws or court orders
- **Association Leadership**: Board members access member data for legitimate purposes

## Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@centrale-ucl.be

## Data Retention

- **Active Members**: Data retained while membership is active
- **Alumni**: Data retained for historical/networking purposes (opt-out available)
- **Event Data**: 5 years for financial/audit purposes
- **Communication Logs**: 3 years for service improvement
- **Audit Logs**: 7 years for security purposes

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Role-based access controls
- Regular security audits
- Secure hosting infrastructure
- Employee training on data protection

## International Transfers

Data is processed within the EU. Any transfers outside the EU are protected by:
- Adequacy decisions
- Standard contractual clauses
- Binding corporate rules

## Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication and security
- Preference storage
- Analytics (anonymized)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Data Breach Notification

In case of a data breach:
- We will notify authorities within 72 hours
- Affected individuals will be notified if high risk
- We will take immediate steps to contain the breach

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect:
- Legal requirements
- Service changes
- Best practice improvements

We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notifications.
